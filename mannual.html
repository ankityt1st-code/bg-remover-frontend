How to Use Remove BG

Removing image backgrounds is just 3 clicks away.

1

Upload your image

Click the Upload Image button on the home page to select a file from your mobile device or PC. You can also simply drag and drop the image directly into the upload area if you are on a computer.

2

Wait for AI Processing

Once uploaded, our highly-trained artificial intelligence takes over. It will automatically detect the subject of your photo, separating it from the background instantly. No manual clicking or lassoing required!

3

Download the Result

Preview the result. Once you are satisfied with the transparent background, click on Download to save the image directly to your device. You can then use it on websites, presentations, e-commerce stores, and more.
